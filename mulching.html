Details that matter

Each bed gets a bespoke treatment

From tree rings to foundation beds, we shape, smooth, and install mulch with an eye for drainage and plant health.

Defined edges

Hand-spaded or tooled edges keep mulch in place and create a crisp boundary against turf.

Depth control

2–3 inches stops weeds and insulates roots without suffocating stems or soil biology.

Drainage aware

Mulch lines slope away from foundations and tree flares to avoid rot and pooling.
