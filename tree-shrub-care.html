Watchlist

Common pests and diseases we see locally

Issue Our response
Bagworms Inspect evergreens, remove bags, and apply targeted treatment before hatching.
Scale & aphids Dormant oil where appropriate and systemic applications to stop sap loss.
Leaf spot Prune for airflow, clean debris, and use disease-specific sprays as needed.
Pruning windows

Late winter

Structural pruning while trees are dormant to reduce stress.

Post-bloom

Flowering shrubs get shape trims right after bloom to protect next year's buds.

Mid-summer touch

Light thinning for airflow and safety clearances over walks and drives.
