Fall & winter clean up

Leaf removal and winter prep made easy

Similar to TruGreen’s seasonal service pages, we keep it simple: clear leaves, protect turf, and tidy beds before cold weather hits.

Leaf & debris removal Final mow & edge Bed & gutter options

Service focus

Leaf collection, debris removal, and a final trim so lawns and beds enter winter clean.

Best timing

Late fall through early winter, with multiple visits available during heavy drops.

Pairs well with

Aeration & seeding in early fall or winter pruning for shrubs and ornamentals.

Cleanup steps

Clear, prep, and protect

1

Gather & haul

Blow, rake, and collect leaves from turf, beds, and hardscapes; haul off-site if needed.

2

Final mow & edge

Set a winter-ready height, edge walks and drives, and remove debris from turf.

3

Protect beds

Cut back perennials, tidy beds, and add mulch or pine straw where it helps insulate.

What to expect

  • Cleaner turf and beds with leaves fully removed or mulched per your preference.
  • Optional gutter cleanouts and shrub pruning to protect your home and plants.
  • Easy scheduling for single visits or recurring cleanups during heavy leaf drop.
